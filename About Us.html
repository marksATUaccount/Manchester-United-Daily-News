About Us



Our Story

We creted Manchester United Daily News when we were watching a match one day and the broadcasters that were covering the game did not have a post game interview or analysis. We realised that not all tv channels have this privilag, and so decided to make a website that goes into detail about all things Man Utd for people that have found themselves in a similar situation. We want all football fans to have unlimited access to all things Man Utd.


Who are we?

We at Manchester United Daily News are fans just like you. We are not professionals in the area of football at all. We are not pundits or commentators, We are fans of Manchester United and Football in general.


What is this page for?

Manchester United Daily News is a Fan Forum that Man Utd Supporters can use to get their daily news on Everythign Manchester United. From transfer news, to matchday highlights.

Our platform offers fellow Man Utd fans to voice their opinions in a safe and respected enviornment.
